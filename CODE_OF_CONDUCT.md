# Code of Conduct

## Preamble

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone participating in the kit community. This includes all participants: developers, maintainers, issue reporters, pull request reviewers, and community members.

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the kit community, including:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Pull requests and issues
- Code reviews and comments
- Communications about kit

## Our Values

We value:
- **Respect** — We treat each other with dignity and respect
- **Inclusion** — We welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Collaboration** — We work together to build something great
- **Accountability** — We take responsibility for our actions
- **Growth** — We embrace learning and improvement

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

### Be Respectful
- Use inclusive language and avoid discriminatory remarks
- Listen actively to others' perspectives
- Disagree constructively without personal attacks
- Respect others' boundaries and limitations

### Be Collaborative
- Help others learn and grow
- Share knowledge and experience generously
- Give constructive feedback that focuses on ideas, not people
- Celebrate others' contributions and achievements

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions focused on the topic at hand
- Use appropriate language in all interactions
- Avoid excessive self-promotion or advertising
- Maintain professionalism in technical discussions

### Be Accountable
- Own mistakes and apologize when necessary
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Work to resolve conflicts respectfully
- Report violations of this Code of Conduct

## Unacceptable Behavior

Participants will not engage in:

### Harassment
- Sustained, unwelcome attention or contact
- Threats of violence or harm
- Deliberate intimidation or bullying
- Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual attention

### Discrimination
- Slurs, stereotypes, or dehumanizing language
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics (race, ethnicity, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, age, religion, or any other characteristic)
- Exclusion or marginalization based on identity or background

### Abuse
- Personal attacks or insults
- Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)
- Targeted harassment campaigns
- Deliberate disruption of discussions

### Other Violations
- Spam or unsolicited promotional content
- Sharing of malicious code or security exploits (outside authorized security research contexts)
- Impersonation of community members or maintainers
- Sharing confidential or private information without consent

## Consequences

Participants who violate this Code of Conduct may face action including:

1. **Warning** — A private message explaining the violation and expected behavior
2. **Temporary Restrictions** — Temporary suspension from participation (24 hours to 1 week)
3. **Permanent Removal** — Permanent ban from the community for serious or repeated violations

The severity of the violation, context, and history will be considered when determining appropriate action.

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you experience or witness a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Direct Communication** (if safe) — You may address the issue directly with the person involved
2. **Private Report** — Report to the kit maintainers at [hello@sandstre.am](mailto:hello@sandstre.am)
3. **GitHub Issues** — If comfortable, you can open a private security advisory on GitHub
4. **Anonymous Report** — Use our [anonymous reporting form](https://forms.gle/kit-coc-report) (if available)

Provide:
- Description of what happened
- Who was involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any relevant links or screenshots (optional)
- Your preferred contact method for follow-up

### Investigation Process

1. **Acknowledgment** — We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 24 hours
2. **Investigation** — The maintainers will investigate the report confidentially
3. **Resolution** — We will determine appropriate action (within 7 days when possible)
4. **Follow-up** — We will inform you of the outcome (with appropriate privacy protections)

### Confidentiality

- Reports are confidential and shared only with relevant decision-makers
- We will not disclose your identity unless you give permission
- We protect the privacy of all parties involved, including the person accused

## Appeals

If you believe action taken was unjustified, you may appeal to the kit maintainers with:
- Explanation of why you believe the action was inappropriate
- New evidence or context relevant to the case
- Request for reconsideration

Appeals should be submitted within 14 days of the action and will be reviewed by a different set of maintainers.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all official kit community spaces and events. It also applies to actions taken outside these spaces if they substantially impact the safety or well-being of community members.
